Game instructions

  • You will be shown an image of a scene with multiple objects.
  • One of the objects will be assigned as the target which the questioner should predict.
  • Your job is to help the questioner by answering his questions with Yes, No or Not Applicable.
Please watch the instruction video on the right before starting your first game.

Important

In this HIT we ask you to successfully complete 10 games. Your HIT will be approved if you do not make more than 3 mistakes. Our motivation for this policy is that we do want you to answer the questions correctly.

Note that this a live game - you will be paired up with another player that needs to locate the target object. Please behave accordingly: do not disconnect from the game and answer questions correctly. Your HIT will be approved if you do not disconnect more than 3 times.

Do not hesitate to contact Harm de Vries - mail (at) harmdevries.com - if you have any further questions or feedback.
