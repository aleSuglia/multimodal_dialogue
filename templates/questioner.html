Game instructions

  • You will be shown an image of a scene with multiple objects.
  • One of the objects will be assigned as the target (but not visible to you).
  • Your job is to guess that object by asking yes or no questions.
  • You click on the GuessWhat! button once you are certain which object it is.
  • All object segmentations are then shown in the image, and you click on the correct object.
Please watch the instruction video on the right before starting your first game.

Important

In this HIT we ask you to successfully complete 10 games. Your HIT will be approved if you do not make more than 3 mistakes. Our motivation for this policy is that we do want you to be sure which object it is before you start guessing.

Note that this a live game - you will be paired up with another player that answers your questions in an interactive session. Please behave accordingly: do not disconnect from the game and ask meaningful questions that leads you to guess the object. Your HIT will be approved if you do not disconnect more than 3 times.

Do not hesitate to contact Harm de Vries - mail (at) harmdevries.com - if you have any further questions.
